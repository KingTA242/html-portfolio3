My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Jeepers Creepers

Best horror movie of all time. The Creeper is one of the most unique movie monsters in a long time. Very interestin story and the lack of a back story adds to the mystery.

Ghost Busters

This is a classic. If you haven't watched this as yet then you are at a disadvantage of the level of joy you can possibly achieve in life. Great cast, great story and beautiful special effects for its time that aged well.

DBZ

This is bigger that just a movie. The Dragon Ball franchise in its entirety is one of the best things on plannet earth. Top tier animations, characters, story and evolution of a warrior at its best. Check it out before you check out.
